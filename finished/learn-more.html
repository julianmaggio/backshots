About the game

Backshots is a shooting game with unique never seen before features, creating a unique experience not found elsewhere.

When delving into the world of backshots, you'll encounter plenty of features that your favorite shooting games won't have!

creating a unique and immersive experience unlike any other!

The game features hundreds of unique guns and thousands that you've already seen before!

And it also features vehicles and unique guns mounted on vehicles!

As you'll always find something new to do!

Some guns offer unique interactions with the games combat offering a more immersive playstyle with skill based combat!

The game also features a plethora of maps, each with unique secrets and plenty of spots and other features!

The game has endless variety and is fun for everyone to learn from scratch!

The game however also packs a free play mode for those who want less action!

and a creative mode for those wanting to explore the limits of our amazing game!

Backshots also offers a bunch of playable characters each with unique abilities and unique weapons, that can be switched out for others!

And unique ultimates for each character that give them quick advantages to turn the tides of a fight!

The game also offers hidden features on every map, ones that may potentially win you the fight!

Guns can also break apart and clog up, creating amazing realism as you have the same struggles as anyone else wielding the weapon!

Truly unique combat!

The game offers highly skill based combat that sports a variety of unique and challenging features!!

Realistic graphics!

Backshots offers a variety of amazingly realistic graphical advances that make the game look beyond amazing!

Realistic humanoids!

As you aim for your opponents body, their limbs will shatter under the force of the bullets, having amazing realism!

Technical issues!

The game offers technical issues with your weaponry to make them feel truly realistic!

Replayability!

The game works so uniquely with a bunch of PvP oriented combat, that it'll likely rarely bore you!
